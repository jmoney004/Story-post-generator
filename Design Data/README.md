# Design Data

Place reference designs and **key backgrounds** here. Every PNG/JPG/WebP in this folder is automatically available as a background option when you click **Generate Image**. The AI picks either one of your backgrounds or a built-in gradient to best match each article.

## What to add

- **Sample backgrounds** – gradients, patterns, or key-art images. These are offered to the generator alongside built-in ABTC-style gradients (teal, blue, slate, etc.).
- **Screenshots or mockups** – story/social layouts and typography references.
- **Reference images** from ABTC marketing for color and tone.

## How it works

- **Generate Image** uses: (1) your Design Data images, and (2) built-in professional gradients (teal-shine, blue-depth, slate-teal, energy-flow, battery-modern, investor-clean, accent-line, and originals).
- The chosen background is drawn behind the title, summary, and ABTC logo. Add or replace files here anytime; new images are picked up on the next generation.
